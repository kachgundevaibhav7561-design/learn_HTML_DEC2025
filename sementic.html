About us

Pune is a major city in Maharashtra, India, known as the "Cultural Capital of Maharashtra" and the "Oxford of the East" for its rich history, educational institutions, and booming IT/automotive sectors. It's a large, developing metropolis, the second-largest in Maharashtra, famous for its blend of tradition (Maratha Empire history, temples) and modernity (IT parks, infrastructure)
